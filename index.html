Lifestyle Coaching

Practical health tips built for Babcock University student life

Eat breakfast every morning

Students who eat breakfast have better concentration and energy through morning lectures. Try oats, eggs, or fruit.

Nutrition

Aim for 7–8 hours of sleep

Sleep deprivation affects memory, mood, and immune function. Set a consistent bedtime even during exam season.

Sleep

30-minute walk daily

Even a short walk around campus improves mood, blood circulation, and reduces exam stress significantly.

Exercise

Drink at least 2 litres of water daily

Dehydration causes headaches, poor focus, and fatigue. Carry a water bottle to every lecture.

Habits

Reduce late-night heavy meals

Eating heavy foods late at night disrupts sleep quality. Opt for lighter snacks like fruit or yoghurt after 8 PM.

Nutrition

Phone-free 30 mins before bed

Blue light from screens suppresses melatonin. Swap your phone for a book or journal before sleep.

Habits

Try 5-minute desk stretches

Sitting for long periods strains the back and neck. Simple stretches every 90 minutes reduce pain and improve posture.

Exercise

Avoid all-nighters during exams

Pulling all-nighters reduces memory retention by up to 40%. Study early, sleep properly, and review in the morning.

About Clinify.BU

Bridging the gap between Babcock University students and quality healthcare at BUTH

Our Mission

Making healthcare simple for every BU student

Clinify.BU was built to remove the barriers between Babcock University students and the excellent healthcare available at the Babcock University Teaching Hospital (BUTH). No more long queues, missed consultations, or confusing processes.

Babcock University Teaching Hospital (BUTH)

A world-class teaching hospital on the BU campus serving students, staff, and the surrounding community.

Location

Ile-Ife Road, Ilishan-Remo, Ogun State, Nigeria

Clinic Hours

Monday – Saturday: 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM
Emergency: 24 hours

How It Works

Three simple steps to better health

1

Log in with BU credentials

Use your existing Babcock University student portal login — no new account needed.

2

Use our health tools

Check symptoms, screen your mental health, book appointments, and get wellness tips.

3

Get care at BUTH

Your bookings connect directly to BUTH. Visit in-person or consult online with their doctors.
